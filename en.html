About MING Coin

MING Coin is inspired by the traditional Chinese culture of ghost money, aimed at exposing the "pump and dump" phenomenon in the cryptocurrency market. Through innovative design and token economics, we hope to bring new perspectives to the virtual currency world.

The Ferryman of the Underworld

Our goal is to connect the underworld with the virtual currency world through MING Coin, allowing all "pump-and-dump" speculators to face their final destiny. MING Coin is not just a digital currency; it is backed by a powerful culture and story. Through digital tokens, we create a point where the virtual world and the underworld intersect, with strong satirical overtones. We also offer the opportunity to burn tokens as a tribute to the deceased and earn NFT cards!
